# PDF Design Analysis Notes

## AlBaik 3PL Jeddah Warehouse Proposal (7 pages)
- **Cover Page**: Full-page design with dark navy blue background, flowing wave/curve design element in lighter blues, warehouse photography integrated into the design, title at bottom-left in white bold text, date below
- **Header**: Hala SCS logo (left) with Arabic text "لخدمات الإمدادات المساندة" + "SUPPLY CHAIN SERVICES", document title in center, customer logo (right). Gray bar background.
- **Footer**: "COMPLETED BY: Hala SCS | DATE: 22 07 2025 | REF: HSCS_22072025" with "Page X of Y" right-aligned
- **Content Pages**: Clean white background, blue section headings (large, bold), justified body text
- **Tables**: Clean bordered tables with dark header row (white text), alternating or clean white rows, SAR currency formatting, bold totals row
- **Sections**: Confidentiality Statement, Introduction, Geographic Locations (with map), HSCS Customers & Certificates, Scope of Work, Commercial Proposal (pricing tables), Notes
- **Typography**: Sans-serif font, blue headings, black body text
- **Branding**: Dual-logo header (Hala left, customer right), consistent reference number format

## Key Design Patterns Across Hala Documents:
1. Professional cover page with branding
2. Dual-logo header on every page (Hala + Customer)
3. Consistent footer with company, date, reference
4. Page numbering "Page X of Y"
5. Blue color scheme (#1a365d navy, #2563eb blue accents)
6. Clean pricing tables with SAR currency
7. Section-based document structure
8. Confidentiality statement on first content page
9. Arabic text support (company name in Arabic)
10. Reference number format: HSCS_DDMMYYYY

## Design Elements to Replicate:
- Cover page with gradient/wave design
- Repeating header with dual logos
- Repeating footer with metadata
- Professional pricing tables
- Section headings in brand blue
- Clean whitespace and margins
- Page break control between sections
- Signature block at end

## MSA Version Final (18 pages) - DUAL LANGUAGE English/Arabic
- **Header**: Company info in English (left) + Hala logo (center) + Company info in Arabic (right)
- **Bilingual layout**: Two-column format - English on LEFT, Arabic on RIGHT (RTL)
- **Title bar**: Blue gradient bar with "Master Services Agreement" (EN) + "اتفاقية الخدمات الرئيسية" (AR)
- **Signature tables**: Structured tables with English labels left, Arabic labels right
- **Table of Contents**: Numbered sections 1-29 with dotted leaders
- **Legal content**: Numbered clauses (1.1, 1.2, etc.) with English left column, Arabic right column
- **Page numbering**: "Page X of 18" bottom left
- **No cover page** - starts directly with letterhead header
- **Key insight**: Arabic text is RIGHT-ALIGNED and uses RTL direction
- **Font**: Arabic uses a different font (likely Noto Naskh Arabic or similar)
- **Company details in Arabic**: شركة هلا لخدمات الإمدادات المساندة ذ.م.م

## Key Arabic/Bilingual Design Rules:
1. English left, Arabic right (two-column layout)
2. Arabic text is RTL (right-to-left)
3. Arabic headings mirror English headings
4. Tables have bilingual headers
5. Signature blocks are bilingual
6. Company name: هلا لخدمات الإمدادات المساندة (Hala Supply Chain Services)
